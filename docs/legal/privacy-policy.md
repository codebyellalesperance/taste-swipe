## introduction

eras wrapped ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. this privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

## data we collect

we only collect data necessary to provide our service:

1.  **spotify account data**:
    -   display name and profile image
    -   email address (for account identification only)
    -   spotify subscription type (premium/free)

2.  **listening history**:
    -   top artists and tracks (short, medium, and long term)
    -   recently played tracks
    -   playlists (read and write access)

3.  **usage data**:
    -   interactions with the application (swipes, likes, dislikes)
    -   generated "eras" and playlists

## how we use your data

we use your data solely to:
-   analyze your music taste and generate your "eras"
-   create personalized playlists on your spotify account
-   improve the accuracy of our recommendations

**we do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.**

## data retention

-   **session data**: stored temporarily in your browser (localstorage) and our server memory while your session is active.
-   **persistent data**: we do not currently maintain a permanent database of user listening history. once your session ends, your listening data is cleared from our servers.

## third-party services

-   **spotify**: we use the spotify web api to access your music data. by using our app, you also agree to spotify's privacy policy.
-   **openai**: we use openai's api to analyze music patterns and generate era names. only anonymized music metadata (artist names, genres) is sent; no personal user data is shared.

## your rights

you can revoke our access to your spotify account at any time by visiting your [spotify apps page](https://www.spotify.com/account/apps/).
